Welcome

It all starts with this deep beautiful sacred practice

Bhakti Barn Yoga

A practice that can be very serious and even intellectual but...what is less obvious, is that this ancient practice can also be so much fun. And this is why we created Bhakti Barn. Through the Asana practice there becomes this opportunity to be completely in the moment. Completely free and not hindered by thoughts - not hindered by anything external, just you, your breath and the moment.

Yoga is about Union and Connection. We created this space to accommodate a new community. A place where people can connect to others through the practice of yoga combined with the lightness of laughter and infused with beautiful color and music.

We want Bhakti Barn to be a place where your discovery can be light hearted and the development of your Yoga practice can be an individual exploration, moving at your own pace.

Come to class, have some tea and be FREE!

-Betsy Davis

Vinyasa Yoga, a form of Yoga that interlinks the movement of the body (poses) to the breath, creating a flowing moving meditation. Along with our core Vinyasa Classes, we also offer Kundalini Yoga, Prenatal Yoga, Gentle Yoga, Restorative Yoga and Alignment Based Flow. During the year we hold series for Absolute Beginners to Yoga and we offer programs for Kids, Tweens and Teens.

We have a vibrant program of yoga workshops with visiting teachers from throughout the country. Each month we practice Bhakti yoga by sharing Kirtan with our community. 2 times per year, we offer a World Class 200 Hour Vinyasa Yoga Teacher Training program featuring experts in the many aspects of the 8 limbs of Yoga.
