Yoga Nidra

With Sherri

Yoga Nidra is an active meditative practice that brings you into a state of awareness where you are relaxed, the thinking mind quiets, emotions fade, alertness amplifies and awareness becomes clearer. First, we engage with the five senses. Then, we methodically peel away the layers of physical, energetic (breath), mental and emotional awareness to bring you to a place of relaxation.

Meets Tuesdays 10:00am to 11:00am
