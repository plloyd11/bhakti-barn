Please come and join us for this transformative adventure as you take your practice to the next level and change your world!

Bhakti Barn Yoga is a Vibrant Vinyasa Yoga Studio. Our teacher training will focus on how each new teacher can learn to find their own unique voice, infusing breath, alignment and sequencing to create an Intelligent, Informed, Fluid, Spirited and Fun approach to the asana. Betsy Davis E-RYT 500, Bhakti Barn Yoga Director, and Lauren Magarelli E-RYT, owner of BeBhakti Yoga Center in Beacon NY (both former senior teachers at Laughing Lotus Yoga center in New York City) and Vanessa Nassif E-RYT bring their love and passion for yoga to this teacher training as your primary guides. They will share with you, all of their knowledge, as they encourage you to deepen your practice, help you to be creative in the way that you want and discover the tools you'll need to share this sacred practice with others. You will also have many exciting experts to challenge you along the path.

Experts in Sanskrit, Ayurveda, Pranayama, the yoga sutras, Anatomy, as well as many other topics, will share their beads of wisdom. Creating a beautiful Mala connecting the ancient to the present. The Yoga Alliance requires a certain number of hours for certification, which have been carefully crafted into our calendar. Because of this and the unique nature of each individual meeting, you must commit yourself to being at each of these meetings in order to graduate and receive your diploma. There will be written homework, reading assignments, mentor meetings, practice teaching sessions and required classes that are all part of the requirements needed to complete your 200 Hour Certification. Your Guides

Betsy Davis

Betsy Davis is the founder/co-owner and principal teacher of Bhakti Barn Yoga in Millburn, New Jersey, a vibrant haven for yogis for the past 4 years. Betsy has been teaching yoga for 12 years and practicing yoga for 15 years. Betsy studied with Dana Flynn and Jasmine Tarkeshi of Laughing Lotus NYC and became a senior teacher at Laughing Lotus NYC for 11 years. Betsy’s teachings offer an experience of yoga that includes mudra, mantra, pranayama, asana, and yoga philosophy that is infused with a mind-full awareness of alignment. She incorporates music and poetry for a transformative and uplifting experience. Her classes are filled with creative and playful movement, which acts as a bridge to deeper union with the self and the moment, on the path to liberation.

Lauren Magarelli

Lauren strives to live yoga off the mat as well as through her offerings as a teacher. As a dancer, Lauren finds her inspiration for her classes through her love of movement, the Earth and all that takes our breath away. Lauren leads a thoughtful and challenging class, diving deeper into the practice through peak poses and a sense of Lila, Divine play. Lauren bows down to her beloved teachers Dana, Jasmine and to her son Gavin. May we all stay on the path towards the Light and trust the Guru within; Tas my shri gurave namaha!
