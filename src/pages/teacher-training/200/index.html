Path of Peace - A Devotional Journey 200 Hour RYS

Please join us for this transformative adventure as you take your practice to the next level and change your world!

Bhakti Barn Yoga is a Vibrant Vinyasa Yoga Studio. Our teacher training focuses on how each new teacher can find their own unique voice, infusing breath, alignment and sequencing to create an Intelligent, Informed, Spirited and Fun approach to yoga.

Our esteemed faculty will share their knowledge and insight as they encourage you to deepen your practice, foster your creativity, and discover the tools to share this sacred practice with others. Experts in Sanskrit, the Yoga Sutras, Ayurveda, Anatomy and more will offer their beads of wisdom, creating a sacred mala connecting the ancient to the present.

The Yoga Alliance requirements are carefully crafted into our calendar. Due to this and the unique nature of each individual meeting, you must commit to being at each of these sessions in order to graduate and receive your diploma. Additional requirements include reading assignments, written homework, mentor meetings, practice teaching sessions, and classes (schedule subject to change).

We hope you join us for an experience of Union and Liberation through this deep immersion into yoga.

Your Guides

Betsy Davis

Betsy Davis is the founder/co-owner and principal teacher of Bhakti Barn Yoga in Millburn, and Glen Ridge New Jersey, which are vibrant havens for yogis for the past 9 years. Betsy has been teaching yoga for 19 years and practicing yoga for over 20 years. Betsy began her yoga studies with Dana Flynn and Jasmine Tarkeshi of Laughing Lotus NYC and was a senior teacher at Laughing Lotus NYC for 12 years.

Betsy’s teachings offer an experience of yoga that includes mudra, mantra, pranayama, asana, and yoga philosophy that is infused with a mind-full awareness of alignment. She incorporates music and poetry for a transformative and uplifting experience. Her classes are filled with creative and playful movement, which acts as a bridge to deeper union with the self and the moment, on the path to liberation.

Betsy is also a painter and ran a decorative painting business and set painting/backdrop business out of New York City for 25 years! You can see her handy work at the studio through murals and decorative gold-leaf borders

Lauren Magarelli

Lauren strives to live yoga off the mat as well as through her offerings as a teacher. As a dancer, Lauren finds her inspiration for her classes through her love of movement, the Earth and all that takes our breath away. Lauren leads a thoughtful and challenging class, diving deeper into the practice through peak poses and a sense of Lila, Divine play. Lauren bows down to her beloved teachers Dana, Jasmine and to her son Gavin. May we all stay on the path towards the Light and trust the Guru within; Tas my shri gurave namaha!

Vanessa Nassif

In her role as Bhakti Barn’s Co-Director of Education, Vanessa helps to shape Bhakti’s esteemed teacher training. Vanessa’s approach is rooted in embodied anatomy, adaptability, breath-centered movement, and functional alignment. She specializes in educating teachers and individualized yoga. She’s grateful to her teachers: Amy Matthews, Leslie Kaminoff, Dana Flynn, Jasmine Tarkeshi, Sat Jivan Kaur, Sat Jivan Singh, and Judith Hanson Lasater. Vanessa’s own yoga journey began in 2003 when, on the eve of her NYU directorial debut, she was diagnosed with Acute Myeloid Leukemia. Yoga empowered her to play a role in her own healing process. Since returning to health, she’s dedicated herself to educating others and supporting her students’ empowerment through the practice of yoga and meditation. She holds a BFA in Theatre from NYU Tisch School of the Arts. Certifications include Yoga Alliance RYT® 500 YACEP, Kundalini Research Institute, and Relax & Renew®.
