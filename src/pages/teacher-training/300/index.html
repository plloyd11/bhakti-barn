New Jersey's Premier Yoga Teacher Training School!

Path of Peace - A Way Toward Awakening

Bhakti Barn Yoga is proud to offer a 300 Hour Yoga Alliance Certified Advanced Teacher Training!

We’re delighted to offer our 300 Hour Advanced Yoga Teacher Training Program through precious and rare fifty-hour facets, each shining brightly with insight and inspiration. We’ve assembled expert guides to feed your curiosity, challenge your capacities, and facilitate your expansion. Enrich your teaching and life with new ways of thinking, seeing, moving and communicating. This is a stellar program for illuminating the essential, deep, and subtle layers of yoga practice and teaching. Elevate your teaching, and let your light shine!

We are offering this course in 7, 50 Hour Facets. If you take 6 Facets over any period of time, you will complete your 300 Hours and be able to register at the 500 Hour Level with the Yoga Alliance.

We are also offering the many of the individual courses as A La Carte Continuing Education Segments. So if you are interested in any specific workshops please contact Betsy for Enrollment at bhaktibarnbetsy@gmail.com

All applicants must have completed a 200 Hour Yoga Alliance Certified training prior to committing to this course.

If you are interested in this training, please fill out an application!
