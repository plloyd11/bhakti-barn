Bhakti Barn 300 Hour Requirements

Experts-162 hours

  • 12-4:30pm Saturday/Sunday
  • Every other weekend September-May

Apprenticeship-46 hours

  • 18 hours mentor meetings (one 2-hour meeting a month for 9 months)
  • 18 hours assisting class/getting feedback (assisting 9 classes, getting feedback)
  • 10 hours teaching class/getting feedback (teaching 5 1.25-hour classes, .75-hour feedback)

Classes-62 hours

  • Attending 35 hours any class on Bhakti’s schedule
  • Attending 27 hours of class with Betsy and/or Vanessa

Homework

  • 30 hours (1.5 hours every week)

Investment

  • Early Bird - if paid in full by August 1st: $4,4000
  • Paid in full by start of training: $4,800
  • Payment Plan including $600 deposit and 9 installments of $500: $5,100

Application Process

  1. Fill out application - Click Link Below
  2. Send email to set up a demo class and meeting with a Lead Trainer.
  3. Upon acceptance, arrange deposit Payment to hold your spot in the training.
