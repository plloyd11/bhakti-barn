First time doing yoga online?

Getting Started

Check out our helpful guides to make your first yoga experience memorable, whether at home or in the studio!

WHAT YOU SHOULD KNOW FOR STREAMING LIVE CLASSES

CLASS REGISTRATION: MINDBODY

  • You must Pre-Register on Mindbody at least a ½ hour before class to receive the Live-Streaming Class Link via email.
  • Live-Stream Classes are listed on the Bhakti Barn Yoga (Millburn) Schedule.
  • If you are new to the studio, WELCOME! We have a great special ~ 30 days of unlimited classes for $69. Click Here to sign-up. If you’d like help with creating your account or have any questions email us at bhaktibarn@gmail.com. We are here to make your experience at the studio / living room awesome!
  • When you set up your new account make sure to OPT-IN to receive emails from us.
  • For current Bhakti Students ~ Make sure that your Mindbody account is up to date with your current email address.
    NOTE: If you have not logged into Mindbody in a while and you are prompted to make a new account.
  • Make the new account, and be sure to OPT-IN to receive emails from us.
  • Then reach out to us at bhaktibarn@gmail.com so that we can merge your new account with your old account - to ensure you get the email link.
  • The Zoom Link will be emailed to you upon registration. NOTE: This is not an automated email so there will be a delay, but you will receive the link at least 15 minutes prior to the class start time.

JOIN US ONLINE

ZOOM INSTRUCTIONS

  • Download Zoom: For a computer, the link is: zoom.com or visit your app store and download the Zoom App for your phone or tablet.
  • Create an Account: Zoom is Free!!
  • Pre-Register for class on Mindbody at least ½ hour prior to the class start time.
  • Look for the email: You will receive an email with a link to your Zoom Classroom and a Password.
    NOTE - Some emails have been going into Junk or Spam. Make sure to check your Junk or Spam folder. Email us at bhaktibarn@gmail.com if you haven’t received your email by 10 Minutes to class and we will get it to you right away!
  • After you click on the link and you are prompted to add the class Password - Zoom will ask you to “Join with Computer Audio.” Click that, and then Mute your device.
  • You will see our Bhakti Barn Yoga Meeting Room screen until you’re admitted into the class. SEE YOU IN CLASS!

INs + OUTs

WHAT TO EXPECT IN OUR VIRTUAL CLASSROOM

  • Mute. Right before and after class, say hi and see your Bhakti Community! During class, please make sure you stay muted.
  • Share or hide video. Decide whether you would like your video to be seen by the Community. You can hide your video at the bottom left side of your screen.
  • Pin the teacher. Hover over the teacher’s thumbnail, and click on the 3 little dots. A menu will appear, and choose Pin.
  • We’re here to help. If you’re having trouble, press chat at the bottom, and let us know your concern.

FOR YOUR HOME PRACTICE

CREATING YOUR HOME OASIS

  • Dress comfortably so you can move freely.
  • Eat 1-2 hours before class, so your body has time to digest.
  • If you’d like, place an object, image or photo nearby that inspires you
  • We have lightly loved props for sale. Email us at bhaktibarn@gmail.com for more info.

INs + OUTs

WHAT TO EXPECT IN THE STUDIO

  • When making your first visit, please come 10 minutes before class to fill out paperwork.
  • Take note, there is an introductory rate that is only available at the studio during your first visit.
  • Dress comfortably so you can move freely.
  • Eat at least 1 hour or even 2 hours before class, so your body has time to digest.
  • Check out the parking info on the studio page.
  • You may bring your own mat, but know that we have mats, blocks and everything you need to have a comfortable practice.
  • Feel free to call us or email us with any questions.

  • Considerations

  • Class Levels

    Choose the level of class that is appropriate for you and your practice. IE: Fundamentals, Level 1, Level 1-2, Level 2 or Level 2-3. If you are not sure ask your teacher or call/email the studio.

  • Advanced Student?

    If you are a more advanced student taking a less advanced level class, we kindly ask that you respectfully follow the instruction of the teacher and avoid performing more advanced variations of the poses unless offered by the teacher.

  • Leaving Early?

    Hopefully, you can partake in the entire class, but if you need to leave class early, please inform the teacher at the beginning of the class. Try to set up near the door so that you can leave before the final resting pose.

  • Arriving Late?

    If arriving late for a class, we will allow students a 5 minute grace period. Please respect this and do your best to be on time. Please either wait outside the practice room door until the chanting has ended or quietly come in, sit down by the door and wait to set up your mat until the chanting is done.

  • Cell Phones

    We realize that some of you may need to have your cell phones near you at times, to keep communications open with your children. We only ask that you keep the cell phones silent and if you need to take a call, please quietly leave the room and continue your call in the reception area.
