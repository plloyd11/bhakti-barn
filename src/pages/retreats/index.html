Let go of your winter blues, immerse in the warmth and welcoming energy of Costa Rica, one of the Blue Zones, where health and longevity are at their peak.   • Daily yoga and meditation in an open air studio as well as on the beach at sunset
  • 7 nights accommodation at our boutique Hotel
  • A delicious Costa Rican breakfast at our hotel (Vegan Options are available)
  • Waterfall Hike
  • Kayaking in the Mangroves
  • Authentic cooking class at a local home
  • Round Trip Transportation to and from Liberia airport (2 ½ hour drive)
